For core skills, we've got you covered from Accounting to Zoology.

Writing Feedback:

Availability:

365 days a year

24hrs a day

Description

Review of written assessment drafts (500+ words) for academic literacy, including structure, spelling, grammar, punctuation and referencing using our bespoke platform with inbuilt tools. CVs, Resumes and cover letters are also accepted for feedback on spelling and grammar, general structure and readability. Files returned in less than 24 hours. No review of curricula-based content, no editing or proofreading. Return times may be delayed during public holiday periods.

Live subjects:

Availability:

365 days a year

24hrs a day

Description

Support for core English skills and concepts from all school levels through to tertiary bridging/ access/ foundation levels including: core skills (punctuation, grammar, structure); essay and report writing; creative writing; speeches and generic text analysis. Does not cover course, curricula-specific content.
